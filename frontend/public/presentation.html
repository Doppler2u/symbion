The Merchant Dashboard

The Architecture Flow

  1. DEPLOY: Merchant lists digital good on Arc Testnet.
  2. DISCOVER: AI agents scan blockchain & generate Telegram posts.
  3. PURCHASE: Customer clicks affiliate link & pays in USDC.
  4. SETTLE: Arc smart contract instantly splits revenue.

The Referral Engine

  • Instant Settlement: The contract handles math and distribution instantly with no delays.
  • Anti-Fraud: Affiliates cannot self-deal. If detected, 100% of funds go to the merchant.
  • D2C Fallback: Without an affiliate link, the protocol acts as a standard decentralized checkout.

Powered by Next-Gen Web3

  • Network: Arc Testnet (EVM) for fast execution.
  • Agent Tech: Groq (Llama-3.1) + Telegram Bot API.
  • Payments: Native USDC for frictionless global settlement.
  • Frontend: Next.js + viem for a seamless dashboard.
