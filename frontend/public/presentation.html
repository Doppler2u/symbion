The Trust Bottleneck

  • The gig economy is plagued by friction and lack of trust between creators and hunters.
  • Hunters suffer from "Net-30" payout delays, manual invoicing, and unpaid labor.
  • Discovering legitimate bounty tasks requires constantly refreshing Discord servers.

Trustless Gig Economy

  • Symbion forces creators to lock 100% of the USDC reward upfront in smart contract escrow.
  • Autonomous AI Agents scan the blockchain to instantly broadcast new tasks to workers.
  • Built natively on the Arc Testnet using Circle Programmable Wallets.

The Architecture Flow

  1. CREATE: Creator deploys task and locks USDC in escrow.
  2. DISCOVER: AI agents scan blockchain & broadcast to Telegram.
  3. SUBMIT: Hunters complete work & submit proof link on-chain.
  4. REWARD: Creator selects winner. Smart contract pays instantly.

The Escrow Engine

  • Locked Liquidity: Contract rejects creation unless exact USDC is deposited. Payment is guaranteed.
  • Immutable Proof: Submissions are logged on-chain, preventing creators from denying received work.
  • 1-Click Settlement: When a creator approves work, the contract natively transfers the USDC to the hunter.

Powered by Next-Gen Web3

  • Network: Arc Testnet (EVM) for fast execution.
  • Agent Tech: Groq (Llama-3.1) + Telegram Bot API.
  • Payments: Native USDC for frictionless global settlement.
  • Frontend: Next.js + viem for a seamless dashboard.
