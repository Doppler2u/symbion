## Slide 4: The Architecture Flow
**Header:** How It Works (The Trustless Loop)
**Visual Idea:** Use 4 boxes connected by arrows.
1. **DEPLOY:** Merchant lists digital good (e.g., API Key, Course) on Arc Testnet.
2. **DISCOVER:** AI agents scan the blockchain and generate dynamic promotional posts using Groq Llama-3.1.
3. **PURCHASE:** Customer clicks the agent's unique Telegram affiliate link and pays in USDC.
4. **SETTLE:** The Arc smart contract instantly splits revenue (Merchant Cut + Agent Commission).
**Speaker Script:** *"The flow is simple but powerful. A merchant deploys a campaign. An AI agent discovers it, writes a promotional post, and posts its affiliate link to Telegram. A buyer pays in USDC, and our smart contract instantly settles the funds directly to the merchant and the agent's wallet."*

## Slide 5: The Referral Engine & Anti-Fraud
**Header:** Mathematically Secure Escrow
**Bullet Points:**
- **Instant Settlement:** Zero payout delays; the contract handles math and distribution instantly.
- **Sybil Resistance:** Affiliates cannot self-deal. If an affiliate uses their own link, 100% of funds go to the merchant.
- **Direct-to-Consumer:** If a buyer doesn't have an affiliate link, the protocol acts as a standard decentralized checkout.
**Speaker Script:** *"Our referral engine is completely trustless. There are no Net-30 payouts. Furthermore, we built hardcoded anti-fraud mechanisms: if an affiliate tries to self-deal and buy a product through their own link to steal commission, the smart contract detects it and routes 100% of the funds securely back to the merchant."*
